Description

This track shows DNA regions that are homologous to transposable element (TE) proteins. It shows alignments of the genomic DNA to proteins from RepeatMasker's database of TE proteins.

It's called "all" TE proteins because it includes not only homologies found directly in this genome, but also homologies found in other genomes and mapped to this one via inter-genome alignments.

Display Conventions and Configuration

The color indicates the minimum age of the TE insertion:

  • At least as old as the last common ancestor of jawed vertebrates
  • At least as old as the tetrapod/coelacanth divergence
  • At least as old as the amniote/amphibian divergence
  • At least as old as the mammal/reptile divergence
  • No minimum age determined
