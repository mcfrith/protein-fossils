Description

This track shows DNA regions that are homologous to transposable element (TE) proteins. It shows alignments to sequences from RepeatMasker's database of TE proteins.

References

  1. Y Yao, MC Frith. Improved DNA-versus-Protein Homology Search for Protein Fossils. Algorithms for Computational Biology, 2021.
  2. AFA Smit, R Hubley, P Green. RepeatMasker Open-4.0. http://www.repeatmasker.org, 2013-2015.
