Description

This track shows protein fossils derived from host genes, which are often called "pseudogenes". It shows alignments of the genomic DNA to protein sequences.

Caution: although hits to known protein-coding exons were omitted, some might be unknown protein-coding exons.

References

  1. Y Yao, MC Frith. Improved DNA-versus-Protein Homology Search for Protein Fossils. Algorithms for Computational Biology, 2021.
